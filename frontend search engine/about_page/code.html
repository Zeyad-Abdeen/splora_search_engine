Anatomy of Splora

Engineering a New Class of Discovery Engine

I. The Genesis: Forging Knowledge from Chaos

The Mission: To Conquer NASA's Data Universe

Splora Search is not just a hackathon project; it is an end-to-end, production-grade AI system engineered to solve a monumental challenge: the inaccessibility of decades of NASA's vital bioscience research. This is the story of how we built it.

The Data Pipeline: From 608 Publications to an Intelligent Index

Multi-Threaded Crawling

Our custom-built crawler systematically ingested and parsed the raw HTML of 608 scientific articles. It was engineered for resilience and efficiency, extracting clean text and mapping the hyperlink network between publications.

Building the Inverted Index

We processed and tokenized millions of words, applying NLP techniques like stemming and stop-word removal. This data was structured into a high-performance inverted index, the foundational component for near-instantaneous keyword-based retrieval.

Implementing PageRank

To understand the scientific authority of each paper, we implemented Google's seminal PageRank algorithm. This allows Splora to identify and prioritize foundational and highly-cited research, not just papers that match a query.

II. The AI Core: More Than Search, True Understanding

A simple keyword search is insufficient for scientific discovery. We engineered a multi-layered AI core that understands context, meaning, and relevance, mimicking the analytical process of a human researcher.

The Hybrid Search Algorithm: A Symphony of Logic

Splora's true power lies in its sophisticated hybrid ranking formula. Every search query triggers a three-pronged analysis to deliver unparalleled accuracy:

70%

Semantic Score

The query is converted into a high-dimensional vector using the all-MiniLM-L6-v2 model. This allows Splora to find articles that are conceptually similar, even if they don't share exact keywords.

+
20%

Keyword Score (TF-IDF)

For the top semantic candidates, a classic TF-IDF score is calculated. This ensures that documents with direct keyword relevance are appropriately boosted.

+
10%

Authority Score (PageRank)

Finally, the pre-calculated PageRank score is factored in, guaranteeing that seminal and authoritative papers are given the prominence they deserve.

Intelligence-on-Demand: One-Click AI Summarization

We integrated the state-of-the-art Cohere API to provide instant, accurate summaries of any research paper. This feature transforms hours of reading into seconds of understanding, directly accelerating the pace of scientific discovery.
